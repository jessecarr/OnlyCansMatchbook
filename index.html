Course of Fire

Shooters will start prone, mag staged next to the rifle. On command, the shooters will insert their mag and engage each target with one round in the designated shooting order below.

  1. 600 yards large rectangle
  2. 1000 yard large rectangle then 10" square
  3. 700 yard large rectangle
  4. 1000 yard rectangle then 10" square
  5. 800 yards large rectangle
  6. 1000 yard rectangle then 10" square
  7. 900 yards large rectangle
  8. 1000 yard large rectangle then 10" square
Important Notes
  • Multiple shooters should be ready on the line.
  • 600-900 yd targets are 18x24 rectangles.
  • 1000 yd includes 10" square.
